About Me

I am a driven and keen programmer with a strong interest in problem-solving. I have industry experience in working within a team to deliver projects across a range of languages, such as Java, Python and React. Outside of university, I enjoy hiking, travel and photography and can often be found somewhere up a hill at weekends!
